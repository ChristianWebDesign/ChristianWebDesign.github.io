Lonely

by Christian Mendez

Loneliness.

Im in a room full of people.

I'm around my family

and I still feel like i'm a ghost

wandering in limbo

as the bodies around me speak

I stay shut


I become forgotten.

Useless.

A puppet no longer useable

Boring and no longer fun


Left to be a spectator

Never wanting to ruin the fun

So now all I have left is a tornado of emotions

Do I run?

Do I hide?

Do I let it consume me?
